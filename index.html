FAQs

What does “credit-based unlocks” mean?

You control spend by choosing when to reveal contact details. Previews show likely decision-makers; unlocking consumes credits only on value.

Where do you source the data?

From major job platforms and professional sources, plus our enrichment layer for firmographics, tech stack, and activity signals.

How do Hiring Momentum and Candidate Fit work?

Hiring Momentum blends posting frequency, growth signals, and recency. Candidate Fit (0–100) maps skills, seniority, language, and relevance to the role.

Are you GDPR compliant?

Yes. Outreach flows include opt-in collection, consent tracking, and retention controls.

Can we start small?

Yes — the 30-day pilot validates quality, speed, and conversion before scaling.

Do you integrate with our stack?

We export to common CRMs/ATSs and can tailor delivery to your workflow.
